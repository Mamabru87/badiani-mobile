CAKE, BROWNIE & LOAF
HOW DO YOU SERVE IT?

CAKE
- Place the cake slicer (14 slices) on top of the cake for guidance when cutting.
- Cut a slice of cake.
- Place it on a plate with cutlery.
- Always try to upsell the cake with a scoop of Buontalenti and/or one of our sauces.
- If the upsell is successful, put a scoop of Buontalenti next to the cake using the round milkshake scooper and drizzle the sauce on top of both.
- Serve it to the customer.

Shelf life
- Chocolate cake: 3 days
- Carrot cake: 2 days
- Walnut cake: 3 days

BROWNIE (shelf life: 4 days)
- Cut the brownie tray into 12 parts (4x3).
- Place all the brownies on the appropriate serving tray and display them.
- If asked, place one brownie on a plate with cutlery.
- Always try to upsell the brownie with a scoop of Buontalenti and/or one of our sauces.
- If the upsell is successful, put a scoop of Buontalenti next to the brownie using the round milkshake scooper and drizzle the sauce on top of both.
- Serve it to the customer.

LOAF
- Cut slices so you get 10 portions from the whole loaf.
- Place it on a plate with cutlery.
- Always try to upsell with a scoop of Buontalenti and/or one of our sauces.
- If the upsell is successful, add the scoop using the round milkshake scooper and drizzle sauce on both.
- Serve it to the customer.
- Banana loaf shelf life: 4 days

CROISSANTS (shelf life: 2 days)
HOW DO YOU MAKE IT?
- Take the croissant and open one of the sides using a knife.
- Fill the inside with sauce and top the outside with sauce.
- Place the croissant on a ceramic plate.
- Add a dessert fork and knife.
- Serve it to the table.

SCONES (shelf life: 2 days)
HOW DO YOU MAKE IT?
- Heat the scone for 15 seconds in the gelato burger machine.
- Cut the scone in half horizontally.
- Put a scoop of Buontalenti (or any other flavour) using the milkshake scooper on the bottom part of the scone.
- Place the upper part of the scone on top of the gelato bowl.
- Place fork and knife on the serving plate.
- Top with pistachio or chocolate sauce.
- Serve it to the table.

PASTRY VETRINE
HOW DO YOU MAKE IT?
- CAKES: keep them on the cake stands and always remove one slice so customers can see the inside.
- CROISSANTS: keep them on the trays; the number of trays depends on the flavours available in the shop.
- BROWNIES/PUDDING/TARTS/SCONES: place them on trays and display them.
- LOAF: cut the first slice off the loaf and display it on the tray.

HOW DO I KEEP IT?
- Make sure the vetrine always looks full.
- Display the labels next to the appropriate trays (see pictures for reference).
