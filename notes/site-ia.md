## Category 1 · Caffè Rituals (caffe.html)
1. **Espresso Core**
   - Espresso (single/double), Macchiato, Americano.
   - Focus: grinder check, shot timing, upsell extra shot/cream.
2. **Milk Mastery**
   - Flat White, Latte, Cappuccino, Mocha, Hot Chocolate Powder.
   - Include milk texturing cues, syrup dosages, latte art tips.
3. **Signature Comfort**
   - Affogato, Whipped Coffee, Cioccolata Calda range, Pistachio Hot.
   - Showcase plating, gelato integration, machine procedures.
4. **Freddo & Creative**
   - Iced Americano, Iced Latte, Pistachio/La Dolcevita specials, Chai Latte, Tea service.
   - Highlight layering techniques, iced showmanship, allergen reminders.

## Category 2 · Sweet Treat Atelier (sweet-treats.html)
1. **Crepe Lab**
   - Mix recipes (big/small batch), Signature Buontalenti, Sauce Crepe, Italiana Savoury (plain/beet), Prosciutto versions.
2. **Warm Icons**
   - Waffles, Pancakes, Porridge.
   - Stress machine setup, resting times, plating variations.
3. **GELATO x BREAD**
   - Gelato Burger, Gelato Croissant.
   - Outline bun handling, toppings showtime, upsell combos.
4. **Ritual Moments**
   - Afternoon Tea set.
   - Include component list, stand assembly, service choreography.
